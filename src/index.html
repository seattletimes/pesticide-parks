Pesticides in Seattle parks

This map contains information on pesticide usage in developed parks over the past 10 years. Click on a park to see the total gallons of pesticides, the gallons of products containing Tier 1 pesticides, and gallons of products containing glyphosate-based pesticides sprayed each year. Enter an address to find information on a park near you.
Source: Seattle Department of Parks and Recreation
Note: Sprayings displayed occurred between January 2007 and May 2017. The figures do not represent the amount of active ingredient, but rather the sum of products applied. Measurements not containing units were converted to gallons by The Seattle Times.
ELLIS SIMANI / THE SEATTLE TIMES
